Recipe

Chicken sandwich






Ingredients

For the Chicken
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • Vegetable oil for frying

For the Sandwich
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced pickles
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard

Instructions



Prepare the Chicken
  1. In a bowl, mix buttermilk, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, dried thyme, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in the marinade, making sure they are fully coated.
  3. Marinate for at least 30 minutes, or overnight for better flavor.


Coat and Fry the Chicken

  1. In a shallow dish, mix the flour with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Dredge each chicken breast in the flour mixture, shaking off excess.
  4. Fry the chicken breasts until golden brown and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes per side.
  5. Place the fried chicken on a paper towel to drain excess oil.


Assemble the Sandwich

  1. Toast the hamburger buns.
  2. Spread mayonnaise and mustard on the bottom half of each bun.
  3. Place a piece of fried chicken on each bun.
  4. Top with lettuce, tomato slices, and pickles.
  5. Place the top half of the bun on top.
