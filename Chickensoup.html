Recipe

Chicken soup






Ingredients


  • 1 whole chicken (about 3-4 lbs), giblets removed
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 carrots, sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 cup egg noodles or rice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions


  1. In a large pot, combine the whole chicken, chicken broth, chopped onion, sliced carrots, sliced celery,minced garlic, bay leaf, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.
  2. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 1 to 1.5 hours until the chicken is cooked through and the flavors meld.
  3. Remove the whole chicken from the pot and shred the meat using two forks. Discard the bones and skin.
  4. Return the shredded chicken to the pot. Add egg noodles or rice and continue to simmer until they are cooked.
  5. Adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Remove the bay leaf before serving.
  7. Garnish with fresh chopped parsley.
  8. Serve the delicious chicken soup hot and enjoy!
