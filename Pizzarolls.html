Recipe

Pizza rolls.






Ingredients


  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup diced onions
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Olive oil for brushing
  • Marinara sauce for dipping


Instructions



  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to form a rectangle.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese over the sauce.
  5. Evenly distribute pepperoni, black olives, bell peppers, and onions over the cheese.
  6. Sprinkle garlic powder and dried oregano on top.
  7. Starting from one long edge, tightly roll the dough into a log.
  8. Seal the seam and place the roll seam-side down. Slice into 1-inch sections to create individual pizzarolls.
  9. Place the rolls on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  10. Brush the tops of the rolls with olive oil for a golden finish.
  11. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the rolls are golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  12. Remove from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving.
  13. Serve the pizza rolls with marinara sauce for dipping.
  14. Enjoy your delicious homemade pizza rolls!
